About M Square HR Consultant

A new generation HR consultancy dedicated to providing creative, timely and accurate consulting services that add clear value to our clients

Who We Are

We introduce ourselves as a new generation HR consultant called "M Square HR Consultant" having sound, practical and ground reality knowledge of the field. We are dedicated to provide clients with creative, timely and accurate consulting services, which will add clear value.

We work in conjunction with our clients to understand the issues and pressures faced and provide commercially sound, practical guidance that work in harmony with existing working arrangements. Our independence allows us to provide totally unbiased consulting services for our clients.

Our Journey

Since inception in 2005, we have thrived on the challenge of providing a strong, reliable link between the Employer and Career seeker. And after nearly two decades of dedication to the Human Resource Industry, we have successfully guided over 15,000 job seekers into careers of their choice and preference.

In the process we have delighted a diverse range of employers by providing 'ready-to-perform' professionals who are scaling the heights of success, by contributing substantially to the bottom lines of the organizations they serve - with Dedication, Devotion and Discipline.

Our Expertise

Our decade-old experience and our in-depth understanding of the needs, desires and expectations of the Employer as well as the Job Seeker have enabled us to successfully handle the career needs of a cross section of the talented 'Indian Human Resource Ocean'.

Our meticulous screening and selection methodology has always ensured that we provide the 'perfect candidate' for the 'right job', because we believe only in identifying and recommending candidates with the correct profile, qualifications, experience and work ethic vis-à-vis the Job/Position requirement.
