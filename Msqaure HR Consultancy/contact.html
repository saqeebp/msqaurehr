Frequently Asked Questions

What services does M Square provide?

We provide comprehensive HR solutions including HR policy development, recruitment, performance management, background verification, statutory compliance, licensing support, PR services, legal consultation, and training & development programs.

How quickly can you provide candidates?

Our average turnaround time is 2-3 weeks for executive positions and 1-2 weeks for other roles. Timelines may vary based on the specific requirements and profile matching criteria.

What is your replacement policy?

We offer free replacement for 3 months from the date of joining if the selected candidate leaves the organization. This ensures our commitment to quality placements.

Do you provide bulk hiring services?

Yes, we offer bulk hiring and resume services. For Assistant Manager cadre and below, we charge ₹5,000 per CV, and ₹10,000 per CV for Manager level and above positions.

How can I schedule a consultation?

Simply fill out the contact form above or call us at any of our phone numbers. Our team will be happy to schedule a free consultation to discuss your HR requirements.
